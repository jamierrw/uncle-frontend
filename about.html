About Uncle Steady

A space to say what's on your mind.

Uncle Steady is here to listen and to help. Whether there's something you're unsure about, embarrassed to share, or too complicated to say out loud – you're safe here. No judgment, no noise.

When the chat ends, it's gone.

Nothing you say is stored. There's no account, no personal data or tracking, no conversation history. It's just you and uncle.

Not your typical AI.

Uncle Steady may sometimes sound funny, but his words come from deeper places – rather than general internet 'wisdom', his responses are shaped by the literature of great writers – people of the highest emotional intelligence.

Beta. You may experience delays of up to a minute for your first response

Model. OpenAI GPT-4o

Texts. Uncle Steady is currently trained on texts by James Joyce, Ernest Hemingway, Mark Twain
